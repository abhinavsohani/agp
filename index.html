about us

Akshay Gyan Prabodhini(AGP) Private ITI is situated at Kasrawad Dist. Khargone (M.P.) established in the year 2016. It is a private Industrial Training Institute started by Karondiya Vivekanand Sewa Samiti. It is a Category-I institute and affiliated by NCVT & DGET Govt. of India, New Delhi.

Why us

Akshay Gyan Prabodhini(AGP) Private ITI aims to build the gateway to a successful career for an individual. As the academic system is witnessing a major transformation with traditional disciplines making way for contemporary sectors, AGP Private ITI is introducing non-formal technical training courses. These courses are divided in semesters or modules spreading over the year. Laced with an experienced and highly qualified faculty, institute-industry interface and placement support, the college aims at grooming the students with the differential approach of practical self-learning along with industrial interface and a continuous assessment model.
